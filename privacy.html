Engine Eye Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

Engine Eye ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use the Engine Eye mobile application. By using Engine Eye, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Email address and encrypted password when you register
  • Account creation date and last login timestamp

Vehicle Information

  • Vehicle year, make, model, engine size, and mileage you provide
  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) if provided for recall lookup
  • Maintenance history and repair logs you create

Diagnostic Data

  • Text descriptions of car problems you submit
  • Photos and images you upload for diagnosis
  • OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes you enter
  • Mechanic quotes you upload for analysis

Usage Data

  • Features you use and how frequently
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered vehicle diagnostics through Anthropic's Claude AI
  • Analyze mechanic quotes and provide cost comparisons
  • Look up vehicle-specific recall data from the NHTSA database
  • Decode VIN numbers to auto-populate vehicle information
  • Save and display your diagnosis and repair history
  • Track maintenance schedules across multiple vehicles
  • Suggest relevant DIY repair videos via YouTube
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of the App
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Store Your Information

Local Device Storage: Vehicle profiles, repair history, maintenance schedules, and saved diagnoses are stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage.

Secure Cloud Storage: Account credentials are stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed and salted — we never store your plain text password.

Diagnostic Processing: When you submit a diagnosis, your query and any images are transmitted securely to our backend server and then to Anthropic's Claude AI for processing.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI): Powers our AI diagnostic engine. See anthropic.com/privacy.

NHTSA: U.S. government public API used to retrieve vehicle recall data and decode VIN numbers.

YouTube Data API: Used to suggest relevant DIY repair videos. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Supabase: Provides secure user authentication. See supabase.com/privacy.

Railway: Hosts our backend API server. See railway.app/legal/privacy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers listed above, solely to operate the App
  • If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Engine Eye or its users
  • With your explicit consent for any other purpose

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted
  • Encrypted local storage using Expo SecureStore
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Secure authentication tokens with expiration

  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of any non-essential data processing

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Engine Eye does not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact us at jack.evans4408@gmail.com.

Engine Eye is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
